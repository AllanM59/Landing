Our idea :

Our idea was to create a new vegan restaurant with fresh product from local agriculture in the center of the city to compete with fast food. We want to create a new generation of fast food but healty one. It will be really interesting to discover a new type of food.

How to choose?

Actually you will choose between different meal : Fast-Food meal but with good product and no meat. Fresh vegetables, fried one etc Also with fresh product you will be able to create your own salad at our salad bar At the end with our chef, you will prepare your own meal.

Why call on us?

Because it is important to discover fresh local products and to assemble them in a new type of cuisine. This is something that is developing enormously but not yet in our area. That's why we decided to come and meet you.
